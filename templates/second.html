This web application is an insurance charges prediction system. The aim of this project is to predict individuals medical insurance charges in dollars based on their status.

After inputting the corresponding data, a predicted insurance charges is displayed.

The Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight.

BMI= m/h ² , m = mass (in Kilogram) and h = height (in meters).
