WHO WE ARE

We are working on Bioinformatic projects as a team of 3 people from Computer Engineering. Working with big data was a waste of time in our projects. We solved this problem with TigerGraph. With our database containing more than 5 million data, we have prepared a project for the use of existing drugs on other diseases. We have developed the results to include results from various algorithms and drug codes so that both data scientists and people working in the medical industry can use them.
